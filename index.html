Bridging Surplus Food to Those in Need

At FoodBridge, we connect surplus food from restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals to NGOs, shelters, and communities in need. Our goal is simple - reduce food waste and fight hunger.

Reduce Food Waste - Prevent perfectly good food from ending up in landfills.

Fight Hunger - Ensure surplus food reaches those who need it most.

Create Impact - Support communities through sustainable food sharing.
